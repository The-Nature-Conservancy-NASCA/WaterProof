{% trans "Although the user can consult the available case studies, the aim is for analysts to have tools to accurately indicate the way in which water is obtained for a city from water intakes located in supply basins, as well as the characteristics of their treatment plants, so that, through soil and climate analysis, it is possible to suggest the most appropriate Nature-Based Solutions and their respective return on investment." %}
